# Roadmap

Honest and evolving. Ideas + contributions welcome.

## Now — v1.0 ✅
- Real-time, on-device dictation (ONNX, Apple Silicon)
- 40+ languages with **live code-switching** (Arabic ↔ English ↔ French mid-sentence)
- Menu-bar app, double-tap-⌘ trigger, Enter-to-send, DMG release

## Next
- 🎥 Demo video
- 🔏 **Notarized build → one-click install** (no right-click → Open) — needs an Apple Developer ID
- 🧪 Broader tested language pairs — *help wanted: test yours*
- ⚡ Lower-latency / streaming refinements
- 🗒️ Optional: light text cleanup (filler removal), longer-form modes

## How to help
- **Try a code-switched sentence in your language pair** and open an issue with what broke — one sentence is enough.
- Speakers of low-resource languages, accessibility feedback, Intel/older-Mac testing.

_Not planned: cloud/anything that sends your audio off-device. This stays 100% local._
